Bass Exercises

Warmup (10 Min)

Strive for even, consistent tones. Try strumming 3 fingers as opposed to 2. Try a pick from time to time. Remember to stretch before and after, and wipe the strings and body after use.

Fret Walk

Choose different finger permutations (1423, 2413, 4321) as you go through these exercises. Never repeat the same permutation two days in a row!

Chords & Intervals (10 Min)

Identify the intervals across the neck. Hum or sing to get it into memory. Strive to finish at least 3rd, 4ths and 5ths.

Thirds

In this example, we find the major third of F across the fretboard (F-C). We have the dual benefit of conditioning our fingers to find 3rds easily, and recognzing where the notes are on the neck.
